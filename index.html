About Gheleon Labs

Based in Colorado, Gheleon Labs is your partner for turning ideas into reality. With a degree in Electronic Engineering Technologies and years of hands-on experience in 3D printing and electronics, we specialize in helping hobbyists and startups bring their projects to life.

Whether you need a single prototype or comprehensive documentation for your next big idea, we combine technical expertise with a maker's passion. We bring professional-grade skills to every project.

Why Gheleon Labs? We're analysts and testers at heart—meticulous, detail-oriented, and dedicated to helping you refine your designs through careful prototyping and iteration. The name "Gheleon" comes from the Iron Lords of Destiny lore, a gifted analyst and battlefield assessor known for his workshop where he tested and refined equipment. Like our namesake, we focus on careful analysis, thorough testing, and iterative refinement to help bring your vision to life.

From 3D printing and modeling to PCB design and circuit development, we're here to help you prototype, test, and perfect your next innovation.
